 (No 6) View of Garden of Castledampph House. New windows were installed by John James Smyth son of Thomas Smyth. The Smyth family lived in Castledamph House until 2003. The house began as a 2 room thatched cottage prior to 1745. An additional room was added in 1830. In 1871 a second storey was added and the roof slated. In 1914 (as Seen in Photos) Thomas built on an extension and renovated the house. The family lived in this until 2003.
